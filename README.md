## Hi there 👋

I turn data into decisions that protect lives, enhance businesses and improves overall efficiency and effectiveness in any organization I am part of.

As a senior humanitarian information management and data professional with over 15 years of experience supporting large-scale emergency and displacement responses in conflict and crisis-affected settings. I bring deep expertise in information management strategy, UN cluster coordination, displacement analytics, and GIS-driven decision support across multi-agency humanitarian operations.

I have demonstrated experience in building and strengthening reliable data systems, improving partner reporting quality, and applying data quality frameworks to enhance accuracy, integrity, and usability of humanitarian information. I regularly support the production of situational analysis, needs assessments, dashboards, maps, and analytical products that inform operational planning, response prioritization, monitoring, and accountability for clusters, senior leadership, and donors.

I am experienced in leading and supporting technical information management teams, facilitating inter-agency coordination processes, and contributing technical inputs to HRPs, HNOs, and cross-border response frameworks. Last, I am known for translating complex, multi-source datasets into clear, practical insights that support evidence-based decision-making in high-pressure, multi-stakeholder environments, including Syria, Somalia, and Ethiopia.

What I do:
• Design IM strategies and data architectures for multi-agency humanitarian operations
• Build dashboards, maps, and analytical products that inform HRPs, HNOs, and cluster response plans
• Lead displacement tracking and GIS analysis supporting response planning for 2M+ affected individuals
• Manage data quality frameworks, partner reporting systems, and Common Operational Datasets (CODs)
• Train and mentor teams across 35+ countries in data literacy, GIS, and evidence-based planning

Open to: Information Management, M&E, Data Management (including visualization and analysis), and Reporting roles.
